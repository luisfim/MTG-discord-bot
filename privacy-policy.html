Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Discord bot MTG Digital Updates handles information when used in Discord servers.

1. Information the Bot May Access

To function properly, the bot may access basic Discord information provided through Discord’s API, such as server IDs, channel IDs, user IDs, command interactions, and messages that directly invoke the bot.

2. Information the Bot May Store

The bot may store limited configuration data, such as server settings, selected notification channels, and preferences related to MTG Arena or Magic Online updates.

The bot does not intentionally store private message content, personal conversations, passwords, payment information, or sensitive personal information.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to operate the bot, respond to commands, deliver notifications, maintain server-specific settings, and improve reliability.

4. Data Sharing

Data is not sold. Data is not shared with advertisers. Information may be processed through hosting services or APIs required for the bot to operate.

5. Data Removal

Server administrators may remove the bot from their server at any time. To request removal of stored server configuration data, contact the developer through the GitHub repository.

6. Third-Party Services

The bot operates through Discord and may reference public information related to Magic: The Gathering. Use of Discord is also subject to Discord’s own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be available on this page.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data removal requests, contact the developer through the GitHub repository: https://github.com/luisfim/MTG-discord-bot
